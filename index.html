SiratSync – Islamic Consistency App

My flagship product designed to help Muslims stay consistent on the straight path

What is SiratSync?

SiratSync is an Islamic habit and streak tracking app designed to help Muslims stay consistent in Salah, Quran, and daily good deeds. It encourages istiqamah (consistency) by turning everyday acts of worship into simple, trackable habits that are easy to maintain over time.

The app follows a calm, faith-centered approach to productivity, focusing on discipline, intention, and balance rather than pressure or distraction. SiratSync is built to support spiritual growth in a practical way, helping users remain mindful of their goals while navigating daily life.

Why SiratSync

  • Build consistency in Salah through daily streak tracking
  • Includes Quran, Hadith, prayer times, duas, Qibla direction, tasbih, 99 Names of Allah, and community features
  • Record good deeds and grow with mindful intentions
  • Gentle, faith-aligned reminders — not distractions
  • Visualize spiritual progress over time
  • Connect with a Community
